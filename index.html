What is Helping Hearts?

Helping Hearts is a website that tries to provide underserved communities with resources...

Helping Hearts is a website that tries to provide underserved communities with resources they wouldn't access to otherwise. We educate and have users take action to help mitigate these disparities and provide monetary donations to causes that they support.



Our Mission

We are Kode With Klossy Scholars who want to target real life problems. We created a website that is accessible to anyone who is willing and able to learn about different medical conditions and disparities in the health world. The main purpose of our website is to educate and to have users take action to help mitigate these disparities and provide monetary donations to causes that they support.
